Instructions: Risk-Reward Challenge

Welcome! In this game, you will be presented with different scenarios.

For each scenario, you must choose between a Safe Option and a Risky Option.

  • Safe Option: Offers a smaller, but guaranteed reward.
  • Risky Option: Offers a chance at a much larger reward, but also a chance to lose everything or face a negative consequence.

There are 5 rounds. Your goal is to accumulate as much reward as possible.

Think carefully about each choice, but be aware of how the scenarios make you feel.

Your decisions and reactions will be analyzed.

Good luck!
